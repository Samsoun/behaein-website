# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Creative directors, agency recruiters, tech founders, and clients looking for elite frontend, UI/UX, and mobile app development services.

## Product Purpose
An immersive, interactive digital portfolio showcasing Samsoun Behaein's capabilities in Next.js, React Native/Expo, Supabase, and custom micro-interactions.

## Brand Personality
- Sophisticated
- Precision-engineered
- Premium Industrial Dark

## Anti-references
- Chaotic multicolored pill-badges
- Loud, distracting neon-blue/cyan grids
- Generic boilerplate Tailwind portfolios

## Design Principles
1. **Material Authenticity**: Utilizing premium matte graphite backgrounds and glassmorphic surfaces rather than flat dark shades.
2. **Industrial Contrast**: High-contrast, readable sans-serif typography coupled with razor-thin borders and champagne-gold accents.
3. **Structured Simplicity**: Maintaining the structural bento grid layouts while unifying the visual spectrum into a cohesive dark atmosphere.
4. **Interactive Fidelity**: Keeping 3D parallax card movements, physics-based simulations, and gaming elements smooth, responsive, and tactile.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio ≥4.5:1 for body copy against dark surfaces.
- Focus indicators and keyboard/touch safety.
- Scroll-driven animations with reduced-motion safety.
