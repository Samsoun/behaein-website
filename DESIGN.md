# Design

## Visual Theme
- **Theme**: Premium Industrial Dark
- **Visuals**: Tactile, dark glassmorphism, matte graphite surfaces, and soft champagne-gold glows.

## Color Palette
- **Base Background**: Matte Graphite / Zinc Dark (`#09090b` / `bg-zinc-950`)
- **Card Background**: Dark Glassmorphism (`rgba(24, 24, 27, 0.4)` / `bg-zinc-900/40` with `backdrop-blur-md`)
- **Card Borders**: Subtle Dark Gray (`rgba(63, 63, 70, 0.4)` / `border-zinc-800/80`)
- **Core Accent**: Champagne-Gold / Sand (`#E6C17A` / `text-amber-100` / `rgba(230, 193, 122, 0.9)`)
- **Core Highlights**: Crisp White (`#ffffff` / `text-white` / `text-zinc-50`)
- **Body Text**: Elegant contrast gray (`#a1a1aa` / `text-zinc-400` / contrast ratio ≥4.5:1)

## Typography
- **Primary Typeface**: `Inter` / `Geist Sans` (clean, sophisticated sans-serif)
- **Scale**: Multi-weight grotesque sizing, sharp typographic hierarchies.
- **Letter Spacing**: Display headings tightened to `-0.02em` to `-0.03em`.

## Component Tokens
- **Bento Card Badges**: Uniform muted dark backgrounds (`bg-zinc-900/80`), subtle borders (`border-zinc-800`), and soft gold text (`text-amber-100/90`).
- **Interactive Buttons**: Glowing amber/gold borders, tactile hover transitions, and white interactive highlights.
